This pbo adds the ability to 'poison' crossbow bolts with the virus. When you shoot another player with a poisoned bolt it infects them with the virus and does 20 HP damage and knocks them unconscious.

To use it simply move it into the "addons" folder and re-pack it into your server mod pack.

Also make sure to set up the crossbow mod: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2536349467
